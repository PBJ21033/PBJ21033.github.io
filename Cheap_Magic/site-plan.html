Overview

Purpose

Have you ever wanted a site where you can by top quality magic the gathering cards for low everyday prices? Have you been looking for that special card to complete your deck? Well at LugiaKing Cards, we have fill those very needs. We pride ourselves in becoming the online Walmart of magic cards with our low prices. We even offer you the ability to sell your cards for what they are worth as well. So come and visit our site today!

Audience

The intended audience are those who love to play Magic the Gathering or just want to collect the cards. Many players complain about the affordability of the product so my site is targeting those that need the cards for a cheaper price.

Content

Home page

About us: We bring you cheaper and better Magic Cards then ever before. We may lose money but as long as the customer does not abuse this, we are happy when the customer is happy. The cards may be cheap in price but they are good quality and will allow you to build the deck of yor dreams! Navigation: If you click on the product tab it will bring you to a few cards to get more information on. The information will include a description of the card and the price.

Images for the Home page

Products

This is the page that holds all of the product images that a customer could want to buy. By clicking on the image, you can go to that particular product to find the information on the product. The information includes the description of the card and how much it will cost. Normally this would be held in a database and interwoven but for this site I will have only one working image to link to a third page. The working image will be the Inferno Titan card which will be the first card.
